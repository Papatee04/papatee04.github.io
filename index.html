Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 9, 2025

Sky Sprint Labs ("we", "us", "our") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect the information obtained through our website (e.g., skysprintlabs.com) and our product platform (currently Cars77).

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information: Includes data you voluntarily provide, such as name, email address (e.g., when you contact us or sign up for updates).

Usage Data & Analytics: Automatic technical information like IP address, device type, browser, and pages visited to help improve our services.

Cookies & Similar Tracking: We use cookies to enable basic functionality, remember visitor preferences, and perform analytics.

2. How We Use Your Information

We may use your data to:

  • Provide and maintain services (Cars77 marketplace)
  • Communicate with you—for product updates, support, and requests
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve features and user experience
  • Fulfill legal obligations or protect our rights

3. Disclosure of Your Information

We may share info with:

  • Service Providers (e.g., hosting, analytics, email platforms)
  • Affiliates or partners when necessary to deliver services (like responding to inquiries)
  • Legal & security reasons—to comply with laws or protect against fraud

We will never sell your personal data to third parties.

4. Data Storage and International Transfers

To deliver fast, secure, and reliable services, Sky Sprint Labs stores and processes your personal data using Amazon Web Services (AWS) infrastructure, which may be located outside Zambia (e.g., the EU or US regions).

By using our website and products, you explicitly consent to the international transfer and storage of your data in accordance with Zambia’s Data Protection Act.

AWS, our cloud service provider, complies with international data protection regulations, including the European Union’s Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs). These clauses are embedded within AWS’s Data Processing Addendum, which governs the secure handling of data across borders.

We rely on this agreement to ensure your data receives the same level of protection required under Zambian and international law.

5. Your Rights & Choices

You may:

  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • View, correct, or delete your personal data
  • Adjust cookie preferences via your browser settings

To make a request or ask questions, email us at privacy@skysprintlabs.com.

6. Data Retention

We keep your data only as necessary for:

  • Operational needs (e.g., communications, maintaining your contact preferences)
  • Legal records (per applicable data retention laws)

7. Children's Privacy

Our site and products are not directed at children under 13 (or other local age limits). We do not knowingly collect personal info from minors.

8. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect your data, such as encryption and secure server protocols. However, no system is entirely secure—your data is provided at your own risk.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy in the future. Any material changes will be reflected in the "Effective Date" and communicated via our website or email.
